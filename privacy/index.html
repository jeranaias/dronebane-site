DroneBane Watch Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

DroneBane Watch ("the App") is a drone detection and airspace monitoring application developed by Thornveil LLC ("we," "us," "our"). This privacy policy explains what data the App collects, how it is stored, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 BLE Scan Data

DroneBane Watch scans for Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) advertisements that conform to the ASTM F3411 Remote ID standard and DJI proprietary Remote ID broadcasts. This includes drone serial numbers, UA type, operator ID, authentication data, and self-identification descriptions as transmitted by detected aircraft.

1.2 Remote ID Broadcasts

When a drone is detected, the app captures position coordinates (latitude, longitude, altitude), speed, heading, vertical speed, operator location, area of operation parameters, and classification data as defined in the OpenDroneID and DJI broadcast protocols.

1.3 Device GPS Location

With your permission, the app accesses your device's GPS to display your position on the map, calculate distances to detected drones, and provide facility-relative threat assessments. Location data is polled approximately every 10 seconds while the app is active.

1.4 Zone Configurations

You may create geofence zones (inclusion and exclusion zones) that define your monitored airspace. These configurations include zone names, center coordinates, radii, polygon vertices, schedule parameters, and alert preferences.

1.5 Usage Analytics

We collect anonymous event analytics (e.g., feature usage counts, screen views, error rates) to improve app quality. These events do not contain personally identifiable information, drone serial numbers, or location data.

2. How Data Is Stored

All detection data, zone configurations, threat history, and settings are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences and, for sensitive subscription credentials, encrypted storage (EncryptedSharedPreferences on Android, Keychain on iOS).

No detection data is transmitted to external servers in version 1.0. The app operates entirely offline with respect to drone detection and zone management.

3. Data NOT Sent Externally

In version 1.0, DroneBane Watch does not include cloud synchronization. The following data categories remain on your device and are never transmitted to our servers or any third party:

  • Drone detection records and threat history
  • Your GPS location or facility coordinates
  • Zone configurations and alert settings
  • Generated PDF or FAA incident reports
  • BLE scan results and signal strength data

Future versions may offer optional cloud backup and team synchronization. Any such features will require explicit opt-in consent and will be clearly documented in an updated privacy policy.

4. Operator GPS Location Processing

When detected drones broadcast operator (pilot) GPS coordinates as part of the Remote ID protocol, DroneBane Watch displays this information to help you identify the drone operator's position relative to your facility.

GDPR Notice: If you are located in the European Economic Area, the processing of operator location data is conducted under the legitimate interest basis (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR) for the purpose of airspace security monitoring. Operator location data is broadcast publicly by the drone in compliance with aviation regulations and is not collected through surveillance of individuals.

CCPA Notice: If you are a California resident, drone operator location data received via Remote ID broadcasts constitutes publicly available information under the California Consumer Privacy Act. DroneBane Watch does not sell personal information. You may exercise your rights under the CCPA by contacting us at privacy@thornveil.com.

5. Data Retention

Threat detection history is retained locally on your device subject to the following limits:

  • Maximum 200 history entries are stored at any time
  • Free tier: 7 days of detection history
  • Pro tier: 365 days of detection history
  • Enterprise tier: Unlimited retention
  • Each history entry includes a SHA-256 hash chain link for audit integrity

You may delete all stored data at any time via Settings > Clear All Data. This action is irreversible.

6. Permissions

DroneBane Watch requests the following device permissions:

  • Bluetooth / BLE Scan: Required for detecting Remote ID drone broadcasts
  • Location (Fine): Required for displaying your position and calculating drone distances
  • Notifications: Optional, used for breach alerts and threat notifications

You may revoke any permission at any time through your device settings. The app will continue to function with reduced capability.

7. Children's Privacy

DroneBane Watch is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
